A Look at the Maori

This map shows basic census information regarding the population of New Zealad, with emphasis on Maori statistics. Unlike most New Zealand census maps, this interactive graphic shows census infomation on the Territorial Authority District level as opposed to a regional level. Click on a district or bar graph line to make the population pyramid specific to that district. Blue is the amount of men, red the amount of women, and purple is where they overlap. Click on the black map to set it to all of New Zealand again. Select different attributes to be vizualized on the choropleth map and bar graph by using the drop-down menu. Hover over anything to get more information.
